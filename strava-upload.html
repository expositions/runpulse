Strava Upload (Desktop Mode)

To upload TCX files, Strava needs to open as the desktop website, not the mobile version or app. Follow these steps once — Chrome remembers the setting for strava.com.

Step 1: When Strava opens, tap Chrome's 3-dot menu (top right)
Step 2: Check "Desktop site" (or "Request desktop site")
Step 3: Chrome will reload strava.com in desktop mode. This setting persists for strava.com across all future visits in Chrome.
